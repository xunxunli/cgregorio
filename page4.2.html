DFAU Social Networking - a social networking app for the tech-savvy elderly of the DFAU alumni Network

This wireframe was made as my final project for a course I took titled Information Systems Analysis & Design. We were provided with a list of 3 main requirements that our app should meet:

  1. Catalog all their information throughout the years
  2. Keep them informed about meetups
  3. Create an easy method for users to interact with one another as well as opportunities

In addition, designers were to create 2 other requirements based on the information known about the target users and carry them out as well. I chose:

  1. Allow for simple event planning
  2. Maintain a directory of contacts for each classmate

Pre-requisite Information

Due to the target audience being of a senior age range, it is important to keep in mind certain key aspects for UI design.
1. A minimum of 12-16pt font size
2. Refrain from using gesture reliant navigations and opt for labeled buttons
3. Use of a simple, easy-on-eyes color palette for the vision impaired
4. Larger buttons and icons

In order to maintain a directory of contacts for the DFAU alumni, a short yearbook-style directory has been implemented.

  • Allows users to edit their own profiles as needed
  • Allows users to toggle yearbook photos versus an updated photo of their classmates
  • Current user will always be the first contact for ease of editing
  • Yearbook is sorted by last name and searchable through text
  • Users can view the albums that other users are tagged in
